Example Activities:

  • Getting more people involved and excited about a community mesh!
  • Graphic design
  • Printing/posting posters / fliers
  • Events organizing! Start a mesh group in your neighborhood, host workshops on mounting nodes, give a conference talk on the project, or throw a cryptoparty to teach end-user security!
  • Mounting routers on buildings (it’s easy! we’ll teach you how!)
  • Grant application writing
  • Developing and designing web apps that can run locally on the network.
  • OpenWRT administration
  • Mesh protocol wrangling
  • Electronics repair
